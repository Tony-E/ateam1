Slooh A-Team

Welcome to the A-Team!

The A-Team is a group of Asteroid and Comet observers who use The Online Telescope of Slooh.com to track and report on Near-Earth asteroids and other interesting objects. This image shows the Potentially Hazardous Asteroid (PHA) 2021 NY1 in October 2021.

This website provides access to information, tools and tutorials related to the A-Team.
